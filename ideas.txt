This is a todo list app you can have your VA's sign up to. 
Then you can assign to do items to them 
VA's can mark to do item as completed, and you can pay them right from the app if completion is approved. 
Each todo item will have a details page, that lists tools needed to complete the task
Links will be affiliate  
Each todo item will have video and text instructions on how to complete the task 
Each todo item will have a chatroom so you can communicate with assigned VA's        
--- PRO VERSION will have video conference in each todo
